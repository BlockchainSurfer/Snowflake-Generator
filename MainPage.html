The Snowflake Game

Goal: Reach the highest level ever!

The Snowflake Game is a game you can play whenever you want to relax, or if you want to become an unstopable snowflake MACHINE!

Level: When you reach the "Fill snow up to here" line, you will reach the next level and recieve 25 COINS!
Coins: Earn coins by leveling up and filling up the bonus bar!
Temp: Once you reach level 2, you will see the temperature pop up. Every time you level up, the temperature will rise. This means the snow will melt faster. You better be able to keep up!

Shop: This is where you will spend your coins to upgrade all types of powers.
Bonus Bar:The more you click, the more the bonus bar fills up! When it fills up, it will reset, but you will also recieve coins!

Tip: Once you click the "Make it rain!" button, you can also use your spacebar.
